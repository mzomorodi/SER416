Best Community Service is a non-profit organization providing access to local services aimed at benefiting our community. Many of the services are free of charge, and others have online payment options available. Our building is conveniently located in the center of town, and offers large spaces that can accommodate weddings, community meetings, funerals, and many other social events.

Rental equipment is also available through the Services Portal, including onsite catering. Access to external services such as in-home care and shuttle transportation is made available through our affiliated partners.

To make a donation, please access our Payment Portal by logging in to your member account. We want to thank you for your ongoing support!
